Colgate Cruiser

Colgate provides a free shuttle service around Hamilton and the university campus, open to students and the public.
TRACK CRUISERS IN REAL TIME



Mobile App

Cruiser information is available on the Colgate Mobile App. Download from the following marketplaces:
  • Apple App Store
  • Google Play Store
Other mobile devices can access a web-version of the app at m.colgate.edu.
